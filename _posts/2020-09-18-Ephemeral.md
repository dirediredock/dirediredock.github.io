title: "#001 - Coal Harbour's lost kelp island"
---
Admiralty map from 1890 has a glyph that shows a kelp region in an ephemeral low tide island of Coal Harbour
![](/images/Ephemeral1.png)
Modern bathymetry shows the same island is still there, but today there is no kelp in the entire Inner Harbour region, likely due to decades of urban development.
![](/images/Ephemeral2.png)
